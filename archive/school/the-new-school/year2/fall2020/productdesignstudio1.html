Parsons School of Design

Product Design Studio 1

Molly Roberts

In this course students will be given a hands-on introductory experience of basic product design process which includes: problem solving; integration of essential skills such as drawing, rendering, and iterative prototyping of three-dimensional form. In this studio, the first in a six-course core studio sequence, students will begin to identify where and how design can act. Students will be working both individually and collaboratively, in a studio environment.

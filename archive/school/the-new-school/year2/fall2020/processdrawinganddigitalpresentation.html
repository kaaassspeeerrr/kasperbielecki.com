Parsons School of Design

Process Drawing and Digital Presentation

Reid Schlegel

This course aims to teach the manual and digital skills that will allow students to quickly produce essential types of drawings required at each stage of the product design process. This seminal introductory course works towards persuasive and clear communication in the core studios. Work will be done individually and includes one-on-one support as well as group pin-ups.
